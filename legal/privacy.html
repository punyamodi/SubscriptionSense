Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2024

Summary: SubSync is designed with privacy first. Your subscription data is stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to create an account, your data is encrypted and synced securely using Firebase. We never sell your data.

1. Introduction

SubSync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Subscription Data: Information about your subscriptions including names, amounts, billing cycles, and renewal dates. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to the cloud if you have an account.
  • Preferences: Your app settings such as currency, language, and notification preferences.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage to improve our service.
  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, we may receive diagnostic information to help fix issues.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment card numbers or bank account details
  • Passwords to your subscription services
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the SubSync application
  • Sync your data across devices (if you have an account)
  • Send renewal reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all your subscription data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly create an account for cloud sync.

4.2 Cloud Storage

If you create an account, your data is synced to Google Firebase, which provides enterprise-grade security including:

  • Data encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Data encryption at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security audits

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

5. Third-Party Services

SubSync uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements in the free version. Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo: For app updates and push notifications. Expo Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export your data in JSON format
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and personalized ads

To exercise these rights, use the settings within the app or contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

SubSync is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing SubSync from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using SubSync, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
